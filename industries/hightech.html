Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about electronics logistics.

How do you prevent ESD damage during shipping?

We use ESD-safe anti-static bags, conductive foam inserts, and grounded packaging materials. Our handlers follow strict ESD protocols including wrist straps and grounding mats. All electronic components are packed in Faraday-shielded containers when required.

What insurance coverage do you provide for high-value electronics?

We offer all-risk cargo insurance covering 110% of declared value. Coverage includes theft, damage, loss, and transit delays. Claims are processed within 7 business days with full documentation support.

Can you handle lithium battery shipments?

Yes. We comply with IATA DGR Section II for lithium batteries (UN3481/UN3091). Proper labeling, documentation (DGD), and packaging per Packing Instructions 965-970. Both air and ocean freight options available.

Do you handle BIS certification for electronics imports to India?

We coordinate BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) certification, FCC compliance documentation, CE marking, and RoHS/WEEE declarations. Our regulatory team tracks changing compliance requirements across all destination markets.
