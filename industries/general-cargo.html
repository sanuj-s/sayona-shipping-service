Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about general cargo logistics.

Can you ship hazardous materials?

Yes. We handle all 9 IMDG classes of dangerous goods — flammable, corrosive, toxic, oxidizing, and more. DG-classified, packed in UN-certified packaging, properly labeled, with DG declarations and MSDS. Our certified DG team coordinates vessel stowage with shipping lines.

What if my cargo doesn't fit standard containers?

We offer flat-rack, open-top, and platform containers for oversized loads. For ultra-heavy items, we arrange RO-RO and heavy-lift vessel bookings with crane operations up to 500MT. Complete route surveys and lashing plans included.

Do you handle e-commerce fulfillment?

Yes. Complete pick-pack-ship fulfillment for Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify, and other marketplaces. Branded packaging, returns handling, and multi-channel inventory management with real-time stock visibility.

Can you help with duty optimization?

Absolutely. We optimize HS classification, leverage FTA/PTA benefits (SAFTA, ASEAN, India-Japan CEPA, India-Korea CEPA), and structure shipments for duty drawback and GST refund eligibility. Our customs team reviews every shipment for savings.
