Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about automotive logistics.

Can you handle lithium-ion EV battery shipments?

Yes. We comply with UN38.3 testing standards and IMDG/IATA DGR for lithium battery transport (UN3481/UN3091). Proper DG classification, certified packaging, hazmat documentation, and DG-trained handlers. Both ocean and air freight options available for battery packs and modules.

How do you ensure JIT delivery accuracy?

Our JIT system uses EDI integration with OEM production schedules, GPS-tracked vehicles, and buffer stock management at local cross-dock facilities. Contingency routes and backup carriers ensure 99.8% on-time delivery even during disruptions.

Do you handle CKD/SKD assembly kits?

Absolutely. We manage BOM-verified CKD/SKD packing with part-number sequencing, line-set configuration, and container optimization. Each kit is packed for unboxing directly onto the assembly line, minimizing plant-side handling.

What corrosion protection do you use for metal parts?

VCI (Vapor Corrosion Inhibitor) films, papers, and emitters for ferrous and non-ferrous components. Desiccants for humidity control, vacuum-sealed barrier bags for precision machined surfaces, and rust-preventive oil coating for castings and forgings.
