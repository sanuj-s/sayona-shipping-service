Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about agricultural product export logistics.

How do you maintain cold-chain for perishable produce?

We use reefer containers with real-time temperature monitoring (IoT sensors). Temperature logs are maintained throughout transit and shared with buyers. Pre-cooling at origin warehouse and cold-chain trucks ensure unbroken temperature control from farm to destination.

What fumigation services do you provide?

We coordinate methyl bromide fumigation for grains/pulses, phosphine treatment for stored products, and heat treatment for wood packaging material. All treatments come with internationally recognized certificates acceptable in destination countries.

Can you handle organic-certified agricultural exports?

Yes. We handle NPOP (India), NOP (US), and EU Organic certified products. Our handling chain maintains organic certification with dedicated clean containers, separate storage, and full traceability documentation from farm to buyer.

What documentation is needed for food exports?

Key documents include Phytosanitary Certificate, Health Certificate, APEDA RCMC, FSSAI license, Certificate of Origin, fumigation certificate, and lab test reports. For US: FDA registration and FSVP. For EU: TRACES certificate. We handle all procurement.
