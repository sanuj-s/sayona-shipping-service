Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about textile and apparel logistics.

How do you prevent moisture damage to yarn and fabric shipments?

Each container is lined with kraft paper barriers, fitted with silica gel desiccants (1kg per CBM minimum), and uses rain guards. Yarn cones are individually wrapped in moisture-barrier polybags, fabric rolls in protective poly. We maintain less than 0.1% moisture damage rate.

Do you handle GOH (Garment-on-Hanger) shipments?

Yes. We offer GOH container services with steel rail systems, garment covers, and humidity control. Ideal for suits, dresses, and formal wear needing wrinkle-free delivery. Our GOH containers accommodate up to 5,000 pieces depending on garment type.

Which textile hubs do you serve?

We cover all major Indian hubs: Tirupur & Coimbatore (knitwear/yarn), Karur & Erode (home textiles/towels), Surat (synthetics/silk), Panipat (blankets/rugs), Ahmedabad (cotton), Bangalore & Noida (garments), and Ludhiana (woolen). Direct pickup from all.

Do you comply with retail buyer routing guides?

Yes. We follow routing guides from Walmart, Target, H&M, Zara/Inditex, IKEA, Primark, and C&A. Includes EDI/ASN, specific carton specs, label placement, and carrier selection per buyer requirements. Also handle BSCI, WRAP, SEDEX, GOTS, and Oeko-Tex documentation.

Can you handle organic-certified textiles?

Yes. We handle GOTS, OCS, and Oeko-Tex certified products with separate storage, clean containers, and full traceability documentation to maintain certification integrity throughout the supply chain.
