Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about pharmaceutical logistics.

How do you maintain cold-chain integrity for vaccines?

We use validated reefer containers with IoT temperature loggers providing real-time data. Active cooling systems maintain 2-8°C or -20°C as required. Pre-qualified insulated shippers with phase-change materials for last-mile. Temperature excursion alerts trigger immediate rerouting or intervention protocols.

What GDP compliance documentation do you provide?

Complete GDP documentation: temperature mapping reports, packaging qualification protocols, transport validation studies, deviation reports, CAPA records, and continuous temperature logs. All documentation is audit-ready for regulatory inspections by FDA, EMA, or WHO-PQ.

Can you handle controlled substances?

Yes. We handle Schedule H, X, and narcotic drugs with additional security protocols — secured warehousing, chain-of-custody documentation, tamper-evident packaging, and compliance with NDPS Act and INCB requirements. All handling staff are security-verified.

Do you handle pharma exports to regulated markets like US and EU?

Absolutely. We coordinate FDA registration, EU MAH requirements, WHO prequalification documentation. Our team handles Drug Master File references, Certificates of Pharmaceutical Product (CPP), and country-specific import licenses for 60+ regulated markets.

What happens if there's a temperature excursion?

Our IoT systems send automated alerts within minutes of an excursion. Our SOP includes immediate investigation, root cause analysis, product impact assessment per manufacturer stability data, and a formal deviation report. The quality team decides on product disposition per GDP requirements.
